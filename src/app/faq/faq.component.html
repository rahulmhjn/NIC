Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can avail NIC Cloud Services
    NIC is providing Cloud services to the Government Departments, State Government Departments and District Administrations.
  • How can we register for NIC Cloud Services
    Submit your request on "Get NIC Cloud Services" section on the home page.
  • What are the pre-requisite for initial registration of NIC Cloud Services
    You should have Government Email account with domain Gov.in/Nic.in.
  • We are a government department whom we can approach for NIC Cloud Services
    Government department can approach to NIC Cloud Coordinators. A list of NIC Cloud Coordinators is available here.
  • What are the pre-requisite for NIC Cloud Services
    You should be registered with NIC Cloud Services and to access you should be having NIC's private secure network (Virtual Private Network).
  • How can we get NIC Email Services (with domain Gov.in/Nic.in)?
    Please browse website https://mail.gov.in for more details on NIC Email Services. You can also take the help of NIC Cloud Coordinator for your Ministry/Department/State.
